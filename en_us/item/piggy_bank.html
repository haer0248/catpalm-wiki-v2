# Deposit

Place on the ground to collect Cat Coins and trigger a block update above it.

# Withdraw

You can only hold currency or air in your main hand. Right-clicking the coin bank will incrementally withdraw Copper Coins/Iron Coins/Silver Coins/Gold Coins/Star Coins in quantities of 1/5/20.

# Destroy

Drops all Cat Coins and consumes the coin bank.
